Our Story

Dedicated to connecting people with nature through sustainable, high-quality products since 2018.

Our Mission

At Treevaq, our mission is to inspire a deep connection between people and nature through our carefully curated eco-friendly products. We aim to make sustainable living accessible, enjoyable, and impactful for everyone.

For every purchase made, we plant a tree in partnership with global reforestation initiatives, ensuring that our business growth directly contributes to environmental restoration.

Our Vision

We envision a world where sustainable choices are the standard, not the exception. Where consumers understand the impact of their purchasing decisions and businesses take responsibility for their environmental footprint.

By 2030, we aim to have contributed to planting 10 million trees globally and to have helped thousands of households transition to more sustainable lifestyles.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We prioritize sustainable materials, ethical sourcing, and eco-friendly practices in everything we do, from product creation to shipping.

Community

We believe in building and nurturing a community of environmentally conscious individuals working together for positive change.

Innovation

We continuously seek new, creative solutions to environmental challenges through product innovation and business practices.

Transparency

We maintain honest communication about our products, processes, and impact, embracing accountability in all aspects of our business.

Compassion

We approach our work with care for our planet, its diverse ecosystems, and all the people affected by environmental changes.

Global Perspective

We recognize that environmental issues transcend borders and commit to solutions that consider global implications.

Our Journey

2018

The Beginning

Treevaq was founded by a group of environmental enthusiasts in a small garage in Portland, Oregon. Our first product line included just 5 items made from reclaimed materials.

Growth & Expansion

Community & Impact

Today, Treevaq ships to over 50 countries, partners with environmental organizations worldwide, and continues to innovate in the sustainable products space, earning numerous industry awards for our commitment to the planet.
